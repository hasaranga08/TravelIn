9 Days Tour

Mirissa, Sri Lanka

(1,186 Reviews)

Description

Embark on an enchanting nine-day journey through Sri Lanka, commencing in Negombo. The itinerary unfolds with a glimpse of Colombo's vibrant markets and temples before heading south to Mirissa, boasting stunning beaches and a thrilling night snorkeling experience. Exploration continues with a visit to Galle's historic charm, followed by whale watching in Mirissa and an immersive Yala National Park adventure. A serene stopover precedes a scenic train journey to Kandy, exploring its cultural gems and culminating in Dambulla's archaeological marvels and the iconic Sigiriya Rock. Optional indulgences await before a coastal retreat, completing the journey immersed in diverse experiences and unforgettable moments before departing for Spain.

Day 1 - Negomobo

Arrival in Negombo on the 30th in the morning.
We sleep at the Jetwing Sea Hotel Negombo.

Day 2 - Colombo - Mirissa

Short morning tour of COLOMBO and visit to Pettah market and temple Gangaramaya.
We left after lunch towards Mirissa, a three-hour drive away. Stunning beaches and a place to surf. If we arrive with light we can take a bath.
I propose night snorkeling.
We sleep at the COASTAL HOUSE hotel

Day 3 - Mirissa - Hikkaduwa - Galle

Stay Mirissa and surroundings. The next day the kids go diving in the morning in Hikkaduwa or another beach of their choice.
We are going to visit Galle (The Cartagena of the Indians of Sri Lanka).
Afternoon to enjoy the beach, surfing or snorkeling.
We sleep in the same hotel as the previous day COASTAL HOUSE

Day 4 - Mirissa - Yala

Morning activities in Mirissa. Whale watching excursion.
Departure at lunch time to the Yala National Park. (2 hours)
We sleep at Mandara Rosen Yala in Kataragama.

Day 5 - Yala - Ella

Full morning excursion to Yala Park. In the afternoon/night we went north to sleep in Ella (2 hours)
We sleep at Anasa Wellness Resort.

Day 6 - Kandy - Dambulla

We boarded the train early to Kandy, which they say is one of the most beautiful train lines in the world.
The driver takes us to an intermediate stop and then picks us up about an hour later.
We eat in Kandy, which has a magnificent lake and the temple of the Buddha's tooth. It is also a very interesting place to buy jewelry.
We left late for Dambulla, (2 hours), so we could wake up there, which is one of the best parts of the trip.
We sleep at New Peacock Resort

Day 7 - Dambulla - Sigiriya

Dambulla is one of the most important archaeological sites in Sri Lanka. There are plenty of things to see and do.
Nearby we have the rock of Sigiriya, the most visited monument in the country.
We slept in the same hotel as the previous night New Peacock Resort The day is quite intense with the visit to the ruins and temples, but we can optionally look for an elephant ride, an Ayurvedic massage or a balloon ride.

Day 8 - Negombo

We left early for the coastal area of Negombo, north of Colombo. Three hours. Beach, fish market. Possibility of kiting.
We sleep at the Jetwing Sea hotel in Negombo.

Day 9 - Negombo

Departure to Airport.
From Negombo we are 20 minutes away.
